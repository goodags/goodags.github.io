My Mission

To do my part, there are many things I have in mind to accomplish over my next three years at A&M. Some of these aspirations include being a leader in IEEE, Maroon Coats, and a software lead on the 120-member AUV team. I would also like to give back to my community by putting on hackathons for the local high schools and cultivate in them a passion for engineering (and of course Aggieland!). After graduating from A&M and shedding some tears, I plan to go on to grad school to study artificial intelligence and machine learning. Then, I want to travel the world doing something that makes a difference (founder of Tesla: Elon Musk, #Goals), improve my skills while in the industry and then, later in life, fulfill a calling of mine to be a professor!
